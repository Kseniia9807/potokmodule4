who we are?

potok is a collective of artists inspired by neuroscience, who belive that creative approach can go hand in hand with innovation. We value experiment and belive that locality can be a synonim to uniqueness to the certain extent. We are commited to support and collaborate with local artists and institutions, as well as with well-known artists that we admire.

Building community and sharing knowledge is integral to who we are. We host lectures at universities, particularly focusing on the intersection of neuroscience and sound. We conduct masterclasses sharing our techniques for creating brain-attuned music.

Potok strikes to build a bridge between music and science, between originality of local and notability of the established.
